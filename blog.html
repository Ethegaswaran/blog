Biography

Usain Bolt is a Jamaican sprinter widely regarded as the fastest man in the world. Born on August 21, 1986, in Trelawny, Jamaica, Bolt rose to prominence in 2002, winning the 2002 World Junior Championships 200m title.

Bolt's impressive career has been marked by numerous world records, including the 100m and 200m sprint events. He has won eight Olympic gold medals and eleven World Championship gold medals.

Achievements

  • 8x Olympic Gold Medalist
  • 11x World Championship Gold Medalist
  • World Record Holder: 100m (9.58s) and 200m (19.19s)
  • Winner of the Laureus World Sportsman of the Year award (2009, 2010, 2013)
